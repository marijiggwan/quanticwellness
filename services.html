Personal Training

Concierge-Level Training Built for Your Body

Every session starts with understanding—your history, your limitations, your goals, your current capacity. We design programming around who you actually are, not some generic template.

One of our core tools is the Functional Movement Screen (FMS). It identifies imbalances and areas of opportunity so we can build a program that supports your body instead of fighting it. Many of our clients say it's the first time anyone has looked at how they move before telling them what to do.

Our studio houses five private training rooms, giving you a focused, uninterrupted environment where the only agenda is your progress. Sessions are form-driven, detail-obsessed, and grounded in biomechanics—because the right movement, done correctly, changes everything.

Hot Room Programs

Heat, Breath & Intentional Movement

Our dedicated hot room creates a controlled thermal environment that enhances mobility, accelerates recovery, and supports nervous system regulation. Controlled heat helps your body release tension, increase blood flow to joints, and move with greater range and ease.

This isn't about intensity for intensity's sake. Hot room programming at Quantic is designed to support longevity, joint health, and the kind of sustainable progress that compounds over time. Whether you're managing stiffness, recovering from surgery, or deepening a movement practice, heat creates the conditions for your body to do its best work.

Small Group Classes

Integrated Recovery
