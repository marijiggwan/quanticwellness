What You'll Learn

7 Movements. One Foundation.

01
The Hip Hinge
Protect your spine by loading movement through your hips and posterior chain instead of your lower back.
02
The Controlled Squat
Maintain independence in everyday tasks—sitting, standing, stairs—with safe, controlled form.
03
Thoracic Rotation
Restore upper back mobility so your lower back and shoulders stop compensating.
04
The Bird-Dog
Build deep core stability and coordination without compressive spinal loading.
05
The Wall Angel
Open the chest, strengthen between the shoulder blades, and restore overhead range of motion.
06
Ankle Mobility Drill
Improve squat depth, walking gait, balance, and knee health with one focused drill.
07
The Farmer's Carry
Grip strength is one of the strongest predictors of longevity after 40. This one trains everything.

This Guide Is For You If…

You don't need to be an athlete or have a gym membership. These movements are for anyone who wants to move better, feel stronger, and protect their body for the long run.

  • You're over 40 and want to stay active without pain
  • You're recovering from surgery or managing a condition
  • You've been told to "just push through it" and it hasn't worked
  • You want to start strength training but don't know where to begin
  • You're curious what personalized training actually looks like
