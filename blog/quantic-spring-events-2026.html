Community Events · Spring 2026

What’s Happening at Quantic This Spring

By Garret McCann & Maria Laverde · April 10, 2026 · Back to Blog

We’ve been building, refining, and growing—and now it’s time to open the doors wider and experience Quantic together. Here’s everything happening this spring. Save the dates, choose your moments, and come be part of it.

April Events

Starting April 15 · Every Wednesday

Quantic Jogging Club

6:00–7:30 PM · Quantic (125 E Chestnut St)

A free community gathering to move, connect, and reset. We jog, we breathe, we stretch, we ground. Guided mobility, breathwork, and meditation included. Come as you are—this is for everyone.

April 23

Free Yoga Class for BIPOC Community

6:30–7:30 PM · Quantic (125 E Chestnut St) · In honor of Minority Health Month

A space to restore, reconnect, and feel supported. This class is offered with intention, respect, and love—creating space for healing, presence, and community.

April 26

The Beauty Boost Asheville — Fitness Sampler

10:00 AM–1:30 PM · The Restoration Hotel · $25

We’ll be showcasing Quantic in motion. Come experience our approach—where science meets soul, and every movement is designed with purpose. Back-to-back 25-minute tastes of fitness from studios across Asheville. Take as many classes as you want. Grab your ticket →

April 30 · Stress Awareness Month

Breathwork & Movement: Release & Reset

6:30–7:30 PM · Quantic (125 E Chestnut St)

Your invitation to let go. Through guided breathwork and intentional movement, we’ll create space—physically, mentally, energetically. You’ll leave lighter, clearer, and more connected to yourself.

May Events

May 2 · Grand Opening

Quantic Wellness Collective — Grand Opening

10:00 AM–12:00 PM · 125 E Chestnut St, Suite 2, Asheville

This is the moment. Our official opening. Our love letter to this community. Come step inside Quantic. Experience the energy. Meet the vision. Move with us. Open house, live experiences, snacks, music, and space to connect.

May 17

Quantic Beats: Movement & Rhythm with DJ Reality

10:00 AM–12:00 PM · Urban Orchard

Movement meets music. Wellness meets rhythm. A full Quantic experience—meditation, mobility, strength, flow—all elevated by a live DJ set that moves with you.

And keep your eyes open around Asheville—Garret and María will be popping up around the city with VIPR in unexpected places where people gather to move. No schedule. No warning. Just energy. If you see us, join us.

Come Be Part of It

Whether you’re joining a community run, stepping into your first session, or celebrating our Grand Opening—we’re building something meaningful and we want you in it.
